About Me

Growing up as a DACA recipient, I learned early on the value of hard work, resilience, community, and resourcefulness. With these core values in mind, I started a cleaning business with my parents and put myself through college as a biologist.

When life threw a pandemic-sized curveball at us, I discovered a passion for software engineering, wherein I live by those same core values. Exercising my curiosity and hunger for learning new concepts; while applying the leadership, communication, and research skills I’ve gathered through these experiences.

My name is Marcos Cardoso, a proud son of immigrants on a mission to empower your business and our communities with world-class solutions.
